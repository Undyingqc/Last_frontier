Server Rules

We believe in fair play and a friendly environment. Here’s what we expect from all players:

  • No griefing. Respect other players’ bases and resources.
  • Strictly PvE. Do not attack other players or their structures.
  • No stealing or unauthorized entry into others' bases.
  • Be respectful and use appropriate language in chat.
  • Exploits and cheating will not be tolerated.
  • Environmental conservation: avoid building in key resource areas.

About Us

We are a team of dedicated moderators committed to ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience on Last Frontier. We actively monitor the server to maintain fairness and provide support as needed.
